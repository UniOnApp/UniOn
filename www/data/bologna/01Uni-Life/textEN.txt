<p>The University of Bologna was founded in 1088 and is the oldest university in the Western World. Around 85,000 students&nbsp;are enrolled in programmes run by 11 different Schools&nbsp;and can take part in the activities managed by 33 Departments&nbsp;and 12 Research Centres (data from AY 2017-2018), distributed across the University&nbsp;campuses. The academic year&nbsp;begins in November. Alongside the highly prestigious historic-artistic buildings, the many libraries and study rooms, many other facilities are available to students to enjoy themselves, spend time with friends&nbsp;and live university life to the full.</p>
