<p>Each neighbourhood usually has its characteristic street names, often connected with local history or nature.</p>
<p>Street names can refer to people who’ve lived in the neighbourhood, the cultural heritage of the area or the typical fauna and flora. In Rovaniemi, there’s a neighbourhood with street names referring to the logging tradition, <span style="text-decoration: underline;">uitto</span> in Finnish. Another neighbourhood is connected with the Lappish wilderness.</p>
<p>Thematic street names and areas make it easier to remember where a certain street is located. As you become settled in Rovaniemi and make new friends, it will be easy for you to understand the approximate location of your friend’s place just by the street name.</p>
