Services & Needs<p>Do you need a haircut? What if there&rsquo;s an emergency? If you need to see a doctor, what should you do?</p>
<p>Rest assured, help is nearby! Rovaniemi has all the services you need and they work well, indeed. The general emergency phone number, <strong>h&auml;t&auml;numero</strong>, is 112. The fire department, <strong>palokunta</strong>, is always prepared, and so are the police, <strong>poliisi</strong>, and the central hospital, <strong>keskussairaala</strong>.</p>
<p>Services such as hair salons, for example, may be a bit on the expensive side, but you can compensate for any losses in liquidity by working part-time &ndash; just make sure you visit the tax office, <strong>verotoimisto</strong>, to get your paperwork done! Local tourism companies appreciate part-time workers with good language skills and a great attitude. Naturally, there are low-cost services as well, such as ESN student events or student sports.</p>
