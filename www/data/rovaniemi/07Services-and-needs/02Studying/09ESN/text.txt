<p>SN Finland, or more officially Erasmus Student Network Finland, is a national student organisation belonging to the Erasmus Student Network that operates in some 40 countries.</p>
<p>Basically, ESN Finland represents the interests of international students in Finland: it negotiates national partnerships, meets with other student organisations, arranges events and supports its local sections.</p>
<p>The local section in Rovaniemi is <em>ESN Lapland</em>. For updates on what&rsquo;s going on, like them on Facebook or visit their <a href="https://esnlapland.eu/">website</a>!</p>
