<p>Santa Claus&rsquo; Main Post Office is the only official Santa Claus&rsquo; post office in the world. It&rsquo;s located at the Arctic Circle, some eight kilometers north of the centre of Rovaniemi. You can visit the post office every day of the year. The staff members are elves and they can greet you in seven different languages.</p>
<p>What makes it different from other post offices is not just its location or elves, but a real letter from Santa. In this post office, you can order a letter that the recipient will receive just before Christmas! <br /> <br /> Santa Claus receives almost half a million letters from all around the world every year. Since 1985, Santa Claus has received approximately 17 million letters from almost 200 countries! </p>
