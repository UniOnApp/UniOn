<p><a href="http://www.arcticcentre.org/EN">The Arctic Centre</a>&nbsp;is a separate department of the University of Lapland, and it&rsquo;s based in the Arktikum. The research they do there focuses on the interaction between people and the environment. The AC&rsquo;s expertise is highly valued and used in Finland and abroad.</p>
<p>The AC has an exhibition and a science library in the Arktikum. The icing on the cake is their multivision show about the northern lights and the exhibition in the Provincial Museum of Lapland.</p>
<p>Go there to explore and learn more!</p>
