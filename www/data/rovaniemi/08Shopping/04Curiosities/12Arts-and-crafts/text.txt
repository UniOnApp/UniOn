<p>Feel like making all your Christmas cards yourself this year? Or perhaps trying out the latest handicraft technique your favourite blogger suggested? Still haven&rsquo;t found the perfect <em>passepartout</em> for your innovative graphics?</p>
<p>Not to worry, there are places in Rovaniemi, where no arts or crafts are unheard of. One of the most popular ones, <strong>Taidekehyst&auml;m&ouml; Hyv&auml;rinen</strong>, has been around since 1975 to serve any artists and artists-in-the-making alike. Their expertise is covered in a fair amount of old-fashioned customer service, and best of all: they offer a student discount of 10 per cent for arts and crafts supplies.</p>
