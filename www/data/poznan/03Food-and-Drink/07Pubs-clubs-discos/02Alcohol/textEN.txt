<p>You must have heard that Poland is renowned for vodka production and its consumption is very high. Although the first statement is undeniable, as Poland is in fact the producer of numerous worldly acclaimed vodkas, the widely held opinion that Poles drink a lot of vodka is no longer true. Nowadays <strong>the tastes and trends in drinking</strong> are so diverse that the market offers alcohols from all around the world. Still, if you want to try a typical Polish beverage with Old Polish traditions, you should taste <strong>mead</strong>. Mead is an alcoholic beverage that has been produced and consumed in Poland and Lithuania since the Middle Ages! If you prefer something lighter, <strong>take a sip of cider</strong>, a beverage with low alcoholic content, which is made from fermented apple juice. </p>
