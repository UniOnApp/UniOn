<p>A similar place, swarming with young people, located in the heart of the city, is <strong>the Taczaka street</strong>, which has become a perfect alternative for the Old Market <strong>overcrowded with tourists</strong>. The numerous eating establishments offer food for all tastes ranging from Polish to Italian to Mexican to Oriental cuisines. Some people compare it to the hippiest districts in Berlin with their vast array of restaurants. </p>
