<p>Students particularly like <strong>Śr&oacute;dka</strong>, a district in Poznań, which for many years has been infamous and evaded, but now is full of interesting, colorful places. The district can be easily spotted from afar thanks to the characteristic mural painted on one side of a tenement house. </p>
<p><strong>Specific place</strong>: <em>Cafe La Ruina</em> and <em>Raj </em>restaurant, Śr&oacute;dka 3</p>
<p>Facebook: <a href="https://www.facebook.com/srodka3avenue/">https://www.facebook.com/srodka3avenue/</a></p>
<p>The <em>Cafe La Ruina</em> and the next-door <em>Raj </em>restaurant will take you <strong>on an Asian and many other culinary trips</strong>. Interestingly, there is a small cinema inside called <em>Kino za rogiem </em>(Cinema round the corner). The restaurant&rsquo;s interior resembles an Asian street. On the menu in the form of postcards scattered on the tables are few, but exquisite dishes.</p>
<p><strong>Specific place</strong> : bistro <em>Na Winklu</em>, Śr&oacute;dka 1 <a href="http://www.facebook.com/NaWinklu/">www.facebook.com/NaWinklu/</a> </p>
<p>If you wish to taste the delicacies of the local Polish cuisine, this small, but cozy establishment is worth stopping by. <em>Na Winklu</em>&rsquo;s menu <strong>includes many homemade dishes</strong> such as baked or cooked <em>pierogi</em>, meat dishes, soups and more. The most traditional <strong><em>pierogi</em> stuffing</strong> is a mix of mashed potatoes and white cheese with greaves (the so-called Ruthenian dumplings), or sauerkraut with mushrooms. You can also try other, equally tasty options like <em>pierogi </em>with spinach, chicken, liver, and more. If you have an appetite for some traditional Polish soup, best ask for chicken broth, borsch, sour rye soup, or <em>chłodnik</em>, cold beet soup served in the summer months, made of young beet leaves and beetroots with kefir, eggs and dill. Prices up to 20 PLN. </p>
<p><strong>Specific place</strong>: restaurant <em>Corcovado</em>, 13 Cybińska st. <a href="https://www.facebook.com/corcovado.cafe/">https://www.facebook.com/corcovado.cafe/</a></p>
<p>When you&rsquo;ll feel the craving for Mediterranean cuisine and seafood, go to the <em>Corcovado </em>restaurant, where you will definitely find your favorite dishes. The restaurant mainly serves Italian cuisine, but enthusiasts of Asian food will also find something to their liking here. Prices up to 50 PLN.</p>
