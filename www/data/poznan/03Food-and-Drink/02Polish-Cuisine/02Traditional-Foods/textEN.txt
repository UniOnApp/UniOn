<p>These dishes are considered traditional Polish cuisine:</p>
<p>Dumplings with sauerkraut and wild edible mushrooms, Ruthenian dumplings (filled with mashed potatoes and cheese), sour rye soup, red borsch, bigos (stew made of sauerkraut, cabbage, mushrooms, sausages and meat, pork cutlet with sauerkraut, cabbage rolls stuffed with minced meat and rice, served in tomato sauce</p>
<p>You can find some of these dishes in little bistros, bars and restaurants which offer typical Polish cuisine such as:</p>
