<p>Gourmands and those <strong>who have a sweet tooth</strong> will surely <strong>indulge themselves with typical Polish sweets and pastries</strong>! </p>
<p><strong>Polish sweets</strong>: </p>
<p>Chocolate-coated plums, <em>Grześki </em>waffles, ptasie mleczko (vanilla flavored mousse in chocolate icing), cream fudge, michałki chocolates, ginger cakes, Wedel mix of chocolates, Wedel cake, <em>Katarzynki </em>ginger cakes</p>
<p><strong>Cakes and desserts</strong></p>
<p>Cheesecake, apple pie, cream pie, chocolate cream pie, yeast cake, chocolate cake, sweet buns, St. Martin croissants, Polish doughnuts&nbsp;</p>
<p>The cakes and desserts are sold in numerous cafes and sweet shops. </p>
