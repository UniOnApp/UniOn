<p>The Castle Garden of the Victims of Katyń and Siberia is the old private residential garden, located on the northern side of the Kaiser&rsquo;s Castle in Poznań. The garden was founded for Kaiser Wilhelm II. It is situated at the junction of Fredry st. and Niepodległości av. Lately, it&rsquo;s been renewed and its integral part is the rose garden. In the garden you can find the Monument to the Victims of Katyń massacre and a bench to J&oacute;zef Kostrzewski, the founder of Poznań School of Archeology.</p>
