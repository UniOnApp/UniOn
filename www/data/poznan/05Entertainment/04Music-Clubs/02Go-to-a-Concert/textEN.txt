<p>Poznań clubs play music to a wide range of musical tastes. There are many pubs and clubs in the city where you can listen to some good music. Top destination clubs are:</p>
<p><strong><em>9feet</em> Academic Center of Culture and Sport </strong></p>
<p>It&rsquo;s a place for everyone. The club is renowned for live music and concerts, DJ sets, company parties, sports broadcasts and cultural events. The club boasts a perfectly supplied bar, a scene, chill out spot, billiards tables, large dancefloor, and other entertainment such as toy football players, chess boards, checkers, ludo, dart and trickboard.</p>
<p>Club&rsquo;s address:</p>
<p>Piątkowska 92 st.</p>
<p><strong><em>Meskalina </em></strong></p>
<p>If you want to grab a beer with your mates, you&rsquo;ll hardly find a better place. Mild music, <strong>artistic ambience</strong>. Frank Turner, a musician attending the biggest UK festivals, is a regular guest player here. Characteristics: concerts by <strong>Polish and foreign artists</strong>, <strong>alternative music</strong>.</p>
<p>Club&rsquo;s address: Stary Rynek 6</p>
<p>website: <a href="http://www.meskalina.com">www.meskalina.com</a></p>
<p><strong><em>U Bazyla</em></strong></p>
<p>This cozy friendly venue welcomes reggae and hip-hop fans, although you can also listen to some good old rock or metal here.</p>
<p>Club&rsquo;s address: Norwida 18A st.</p>
<p>website: <a href="http://www.ubazyla.pl">www.ubazyla.pl</a> </p>
<p><strong>SQ</strong></p>
<p>SQ will become your favorite nightspot if you&rsquo;re into <strong>electronic music, house, minimal techno.</strong></p>
<p>Club&rsquo;s address: P&oacute;łwiejska 42 st. (Stary Browar, Słodownia)</p>
<p>website: <a href="http://www.sqklub.pl">www.sqklub.pl</a></p>
<p><strong><em>Pod Minogą </em></strong></p>
<p>The place where you can party to pop and electronic music across weekends. The club also hosts rock and punk concerts, alternative music, various parties.</p>
<p>Club&rsquo;s address: Nowowiejskiego 8 st.</p>
<p>website: <a href="http://www.facebook.com/scenapodminoga">facebook.com/scenapodminoga</a></p>
<p><strong> <em>Dragon</em></strong></p>
<p>A great venue for an evening glass of wine to the sounds of jazz. Alternative and jazz musicians give concerts here. You can grab some delicious food here as well.</p>
<p>Club&rsquo;s address: Zamkowa 3 st.</p>
<p>website: <a href="http://www.dragon.krzyk.pl">www.dragon.krzyk.pl</a></p>
