<p>You can find a bunch of places in Poznań where you can enjoy your evening, listen to good music, or, if you like, dance. The mecca for the fans of jazz is the <strong>Blue Note </strong>club situated in the heart of Poznań, in the basements of the Zamek Culture Center, in the closest vicinity of the main transportation center. There&rsquo;s a parking lot and a taxi stand in front of the club. </p>
<p>Music lovers can visit the place and enjoy the live experience of art, listen to concerts of the biggest jazz players as well as young jazz musicians. </p>
<p>Thousands of concerts were held in the club, given by nearly every Polish jazzman, as well as world-renowned foreign performers, i.a. Kenny Garrett, Dave Holland, Brad Mehldau, Richard Galliano, Christian McBride, Kurt Elling, Ravi Coltrane, Al Foster, Dave Liebman, John Abercrombie, Eddie Henderson, Randy Brecker, Mike Stern, Dave Douglas, Scott Henderson, John Scofield, Pat Metheny, David Murray, Joey de Francesco, Billy Harper, Ernie Watts, Jack DeJohnette, E.S.T.Trio, Yellowjackets, Jeff Ballard, Chico Freeman, James Carter, Buster Williams, Cindy Blackman Santana, and others. </p>
<p>Jazz, rock and blues concerts, as well as hip-hop parties are organized in the club.</p>
