<p>The International Henryk Wieniawski Violin Competition is a contest for violin players taking place in Poznań every five years. It&rsquo;s the oldest violin contest in the world. It was first held in 1935, the organizer was the Warsaw Musical Association (the competition was suspended during the WWII). From 1952 the competition has been organized in Poznań, in AMU Grand Hall, organized by Henryk Wieniawski Musical Society in Poznań.</p>
<p>The International Henryk Wieniawski Violin Competition is a member and co-founder of the World Federation of International Music Competitions based in Geneva. </p>
<p>From 1957, the event has grown to include the Henryk Wieniawski Violin Making Competition.</p>
