<p>There&rsquo;s no shortage of movie theaters in Poznań. Almost every large shopping mall houses modern well-equipped screens, where you can watch the <strong>newest world premiers</strong>. The biggest such venue is <strong><em>Cinema City Kinepolis</em></strong> (one of the biggest multiplexes in Poland, with 18 screens) and <strong><em>Multikino</em> </strong>theater chain network. Usually the films are screened in the original language with Polish subtitles, while animated movies and children&rsquo;s stories are dubbed. Tickets can be purchased online and in box office. </p>
