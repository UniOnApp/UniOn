<p>If you would like to check out a museum without the traditional artifacts, but a place where you can experience an <strong>interactive and multimedia presentation </strong>focused on the past and the present Polish history, considered by the National Geographic as <strong>one of the 7 new wonders in Poland</strong>, you shouldn&rsquo;t miss out on ICHOT! Porta Posnania&rsquo;s slogan is <em>1050 years in a day</em>. The ecomuseum follows the concept of the <strong><em>third place</em></strong> i.e. a space where people can meet <em>to watch, self-present and bond with the community</em>. The particularly exposed <em>Light </em>will guide you through the three metaphorically-called rooms: <em>Water, Gold, </em>and <em>Stained Glass</em>, to unveil the history of the Śr&oacute;dka district and the Cathedral Island. The museum promotes itself with the slogan: <em>You&rsquo;ll touch-see-understand</em>. Check the proposed theme tours:</p>
<ul>
<li><em>Przepis na sukces. Wycieczka słodko-gorzka &ndash; A recipe for success. A bitter-sweet tour.</em></li>
<li><em>Jesteśmy w kontakcie. Wycieczka pod wysokim napięciem &ndash; We&rsquo;re in touch. A high voltage tour.</em></li>
<li><em>Cuda i tw&oacute;rcy. Wycieczka z wielkim uszanowaniem &ndash; Wonders&amp;creators. A tour with high esteem.</em> </li>
</ul>
<p>Scout out the <em>Fest Fyrtel</em> &ndash; the best form of sightseeing if you like to explore the city in an unconventional way!</p>
