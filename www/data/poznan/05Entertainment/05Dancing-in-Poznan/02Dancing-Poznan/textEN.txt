<p>From 1994 to 2016 the Polish Dance Theater was the organizer of the <strong>International Contemporary Dance Workshops</strong> and, since 2004, has coordinated the <strong>International Dance Theaters&rsquo; Festival,</strong> lately known as <strong><em>Dancing Poznań</em></strong>. Poznań is a city of dance. Thanks to the Polish Dance Theater, <strong>specialist dance workshops</strong>, which are conducted by <strong>outstanding choreographers</strong>, <strong>are becoming increasingly popular</strong>. The Polish Dance Theater is all about the <strong>Avant-garde movement</strong>, unique choreographies, and various dance techniques. Here you can find diverse dance classes ranging from from jazz (modern, fusion, Broadway), to hip-hop to flamenco, salsa, samba and afro. If you&rsquo;re a <strong>fan of dancing</strong>, the workshops will surely inspire you. You&rsquo;ll feel the <strong>powerful rhythm</strong>, encounter energetic people, and boost your mood. Unsure of your skills? Lacking dance experience? Cheer up and choose a course for beginners. Have you been dancing for years? Select workshops for advanced dancers. </p>
<p><em>Dancing Poznań </em>is also a <strong>scene of presentation &ndash; dance performances</strong> of renowned ensembles are <strong>encounters with modern aesthetics</strong>, Avant-garde techniques, and with perfection in gestures. <strong>Beauty enchanted in motion</strong> &ndash; don&rsquo;t miss the opportunity to see the dance performances! </p>
