<p><strong>Art Of Move Dance School</strong></p>
<p>The School&rsquo;s ad points to its <strong>unique assets</strong>: the possibility of getting in touch with people <strong>addicted to music and motion</strong>, the chance to learn a <strong>rare</strong> dance <strong>technique</strong>, training with an <strong>excellent instructor</strong> <strong>in a professional dance space</strong>, co-formation of <strong>dance ensembles</strong>, an offer of outbound weekend classes, and diverse schedule of courses. The <strong>schedule</strong> is divided into three categories: <strong>In pairs </strong>(wedding dance, partner dances), <strong>Ladies </strong>(suelta, bachala ladies styling, ladies Latino), and <strong>Stage </strong>(contemporary jazz, lyrical jazz, modern dance). Here you can learn the reportedly hypnotizing dance technique zouk, multiform bachata, or the emotional kizomba.</p>
