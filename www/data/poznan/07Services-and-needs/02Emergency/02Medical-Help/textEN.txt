<p>Dialing the<strong> emergency numbers 999/112 </strong>is free of charge. </p>
<p>If you are in need of medical help, call this number and explain your situation. The <strong>operator</strong> will ask you a few basic questions to assess the <strong>ground for assistance</strong> and determine what kind of help you need. Even if it&rsquo;s a <strong>life-threatening situation</strong>, try to stay calm and <strong>don&rsquo;t raise your voice</strong>, or <strong>hasten </strong>the operator, it won&rsquo;t speed up the services, but might slow down the dispatch.</p>
<p>You should <strong>provide</strong> the operator with <strong>basic information</strong>:</p>
<ol>
<li>what happened/is the <strong>injured person conscious</strong>/is he or she moving/ <strong>reacting to touch/pain</strong></li>
<li>where the accident happened/address/whereabouts</li>
<li>who <strong>needs help</strong>/how many people/age and sex/ name and surname</li>
<li>who is calling/ <strong>contact phone number of the caller</strong></li>
<li><strong>additional information</strong> on the localization.&nbsp;</li>
</ol>
