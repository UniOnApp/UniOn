<p>In Poland, in an emergency, you can call the European emergency number <strong>112</strong>. The connection is free of charge, no matter if you call from your mobile phone or a landline. You will be connected with a dispatcher, who, depending on the emergency, will send for the <strong>rescue team, fire brigade</strong>, or <strong>the police</strong> respectively.</p>
<p>The operator can give you first pre-medical aid instructions, while you wait for the ambulance.</p>
<p>If you&rsquo;re sure what kind of help you need, dial:</p>
<p><strong>997 &ndash; to call the Police</strong></p>
<p><strong>999 &ndash; to call the Ambulance </strong></p>
<p><strong>998- to call the Fire Brigade</strong></p>
<p><strong>986 &ndash; to call the Municipal Police</strong></p>
