<p>Foreigners in Poland can use medical care services financed from public funds. The National Health Fund (NFZ) offers two types of insurance: compulsory and voluntary.</p>
<p>The benefits of the medical services are available to the insured citizens of the EU and EFTA countries, people employed in Poland, family members of the insured, refugees, the unemployed, the clergy, and children who attend school.</p>
<p>Other foreigners staying legally in Poland can benefit from the medical services once they purchase the voluntary health insurance. </p>
