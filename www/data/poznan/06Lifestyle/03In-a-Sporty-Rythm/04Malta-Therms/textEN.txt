<p>Almost everyone loves water, beach, and summer atmosphere&hellip;</p>
<p>When you go to the Malta Thermal Baths in Pozna≈Ñ, you can practically feel like being on Hawaii ;) This venue is the biggest state-of-the-art water sports and leisure complex in Poland.</p>
<p>The Thermal Baths are situated on the Malta Lake, which lends the complex its name.</p>
<p>Everyone can have fun here. If you like swimming, you can choose from 18 sports pools. You can test yourself at the 50-meter distance, which is the length of the lanes. Besides, you can try diving 5 meters down to the floor of the pool. In this deep pool you can have a go at scuba diving or high diving.</p>
<p>If you&rsquo;re not that into swimming and prefer peace and relaxation to water sports, you can visit the complex of 14 saunas, coming in all shapes and sizes. There are Finnish saunas, steam baths, blossom steam baths, a tepidarium with water beds, snow room, salt steam bath, and many more.</p>
<p>Like to spend time in water, but not keen on swimming in a pool? You can enjoy the biggest aquapark in Poland. The place comprises 16 basins (including a wave pool), and 13 different slides. The outdoor pools are an attraction, too. If you visit the Thermal Baths on a warm sunny day, you can hang around on the outdoor terraces with a view on the Malta Lake.</p>
<p>It&rsquo;s a great place to take a break and unwind. </p>
