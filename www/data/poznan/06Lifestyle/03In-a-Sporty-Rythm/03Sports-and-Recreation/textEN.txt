<p>If you enjoy sport, Poznań will give you plenty of opportunities to spend active leisure time. Poznań Sports and Recreation Centers comprise a complex of a few venues, offering many possibilities:</p>
<p>1. Swimming pool, ice-skating rink and sports halls <strong><em>Chwiałka</em></strong> &ndash; where you can improve your swimming technique, ice-skate in the winter, and play basketball, volleyball, handball, hockey, or futsal throughout the year in the sports halls. </p>
<p>More at: <a href="http://chwialka.poznan.pl/">http://chwialka.poznan.pl/</a></p>
<p>2. <strong>Malta</strong> &ndash; recreational area around the artificial Malta Lake, offering many attractions both for the adults and the kids: playgrounds, ski slope with artificial surface, tobbogan, high ropes course, seasonal bike rental MaltaBike, and seasonal ice rink. It&rsquo;s a popular spot with runners, rollerbladers and cyclists. More at: <a href="http://malta.poznan.pl/">http://malta.poznan.pl/</a></p>
<p>3. <strong>Arena</strong> &ndash; a sports and entertainment hall located in the center of Poznań, a characteristic landmark known to inhabitants of Poznań. It&rsquo;s a place where concerts and sports events take place. Details at: <a href="http://arena.poznan.pl/">http://arena.poznan.pl/</a></p>
<p>4. <strong>Rataje</strong> Center by the Warta River is a sports and recreation complex situated by the Warta river. The center features a modern bowling club with a bar, tennis courts, football pitch, basketball court, and a skatepark. There is a playground for children, too. You can read more at: <a href="http://osrodekrataje.poznan.pl/">http://osrodekrataje.poznan.pl/</a></p>
<p>5. <strong>Golęcin Stadium</strong>- is a track-and-field stadium with artificial surface and 8-lane racecourse. If you are keen on the less popular sports such as the hammer or discus throw, or javelin, this is the right place to train those disciplines. In addition, the place houses a speedway track renovated in the 2016 and tennis courts. More at: <a href="http://golecin.poznan.pl/">http://golecin.poznan.pl/</a></p>
