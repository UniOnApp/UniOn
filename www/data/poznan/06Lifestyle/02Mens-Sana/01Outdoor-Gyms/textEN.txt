<p><strong>Exercise machines such as stepper, rowing machine, orbitrek, pendulum, or twisting station</strong> sprang up across Poznań <strong>housing estates</strong>, parks and some squares.</p>
<p>In our city recreational areas can be found in almost every district, among others on the Malta Lake, at the Citadel, in Jordanowski Garden no. 1 by Działowa st., on Kr&oacute;lowej Jadwigi st., in Piątkowski forest, Szelągowski park, at the Bohater&oacute;w II Wojny Światowej housing estate, Stare Żegrze housing estate, at Jarochowskiego st., Promienista st., Sochaczewska st., at Orła Białego housing estates, at Brzask/Zorza st., at Tysiąclecia housing estates, on the Warta river.</p>
<p>The outdoor gyms <strong>can be used</strong> for free at any time, all year round. It&rsquo;s always a good time to <strong>exercise and keep in shape</strong>. </p>
