<p><strong>ZTM&rsquo;s bike rental</strong></p>
<p>Bike rental is equipped with 80 bicycles. </p>
<p><strong>Opening hours </strong></p>
<p>from 1 April to 31 October:</p>
<ul>
<li>Monday &ndash; Friday: from 7.00 a.m. to 9.00 p.m, </li>
<li>Sunday, Saturday, holidays: from 10.00 a.m. to 8.00 p.m. </li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Bike rates:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>For every one hour - 2,5 PLN </li>
<li>For 5 hours and longer (until the closing time of the bike rental on the same day) - 12 PLN </li>
<li>For every consecutive day - 12 PLN </li>
<li>Subscription: </li>
<ul>
<li>monthly - 25 PLN </li>
<li>season - 100 PLN </li>
</ul>
</ul>
<p>The monthly and season subscription rates are applicable for the holders of valid monthly ticket for public transport.</p>
<p>Price list for the holders of a valid ticket for the network on the PEKA card:</p>
<ul>
<li>Initial non-refundable fee - 7 PLN </li>
<li>For each rental and returning bicycle on the following day before the closing time of the bike rental &ndash; free of charge </li>
<li>For every consecutive day - 10 PLN </li>
</ul>
