<p><strong>Avenida Poznań (former Poznań City Center)</strong> is the name of <strong>the shopping mall</strong> and <strong>integrated transportation center</strong> encompassing the railway station (Poznań Main Station) and bus station. It includes <strong>stores and customer service points </strong>of Polish and international companies. The multistory parking serves the mall&rsquo;s clients as well as travellers. Parking is free of charge up to 3 h. Also, <strong>the shopping mall hosts temporary art exhibitions</strong>. The shopping center is open from 9.00 a.m. to 9.00 p.m., while the shops and facilities nearest to the railway station open at 7.00 a.m.</p>
