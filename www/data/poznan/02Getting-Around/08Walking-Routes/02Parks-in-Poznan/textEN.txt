<p><strong>We particularly recommend taking a walk around city parks</strong>. In the very center of the city you&rsquo;ll find: Frederic Chopin Park, Henryk Wieniawski Park, park adjacent to the Imperial Castle, outside the city center: Sołacz Park, the Citadel, Malta Park, Dębina Park; nature reserves and <strong>city groves</strong> (Żurawiniec, Marceliński Grove, Golęciński Grove, picturesque Szachty); <strong>forest trails</strong> around Rusałka lake, Strzeszyn and Kiekrz, <strong>walking path by the Warta river</strong>. The Botanical and Dendrological Gardens are <strong>a must-see in spring</strong>. It is also worthwhile to visit the R&oacute;żany Potok Valley, see the Morasko Meteorite Nature Reserve or the Wielkopolska National Park. You won&rsquo;t forget <strong>the beautiful sights</strong> while strolling in Puszczykowo, go on a trip to K&oacute;rnik and Rogalin, too. At the beginning of March you should definitely visit the Śnieżycowy Jar Nature Reserve.</p>
