<p><strong>In case you prefer reaching your destination by plane</strong>, you can travel from Poznań-Ławica Henryk Wieniawski Airport. Situated 7 km <strong>west of the city center</strong>, this international airport is one of the oldest airports in Poland. Poznań-Ławica offers more than 60 flight connections to destinations in Poland and <strong>around the world</strong>. Operated airlines include: Lufthansa, PLL LOT, Ryanair, SAS Scandinavian Airlines and Wizzair.</p>
<p>You can find <strong>full timetable of national and international flights</strong> at: http://www.airport-poznan.com.pl/pl/</p>
<p>Poznań-Ławica Airport <strong>is connected</strong> <strong>to the city center by public transport</strong>. Bus stops can be found in front of the passenger terminals, or nearby.</p>

<p><strong>Line no. 48</strong></p>
<p>The second bus line connecting the airport to the center Poznań is line no. 48. The bus stop is situated at Wyszęborska st., circa 200 meters south from the passenger terminal. </p>
<ul>
<li>WYSZĘBORSKA &ndash; RONDO KAPONIERA <a href="http://ztm.poznan.pl/komunikacja/rozklad/#/rozklad/wgLinii/ZTM_MPK/0/662/48">(timetable)</a></li>
