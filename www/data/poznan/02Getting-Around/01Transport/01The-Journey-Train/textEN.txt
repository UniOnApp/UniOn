<p>If you would like to <strong>explore Poland</strong>, <strong>you can use the public transport services</strong>.</p>
<p>Travelling by <strong>train</strong> is one of the options. <strong>Mass transport services</strong> in Poland are offered by a few different companies providing <strong>domestic and international connections</strong>. You can set out on a journey by train to many cities in Poland, as well as to Berlin, or Vienna. Train tickets can be bought at <strong>railway stations</strong>, via the Internet and mobile applications. Railway companies offer many ticket discounts and promotions such as the 51% discount for students, or <strong>special season and weekend deals</strong>.</p>
<p>You can search for domestic and international railway services and check the full railway timetables of all operators at: <a href="http://www.rozklad-pkp.pl/">http://www.rozklad-pkp.pl/</a></p>
