<p><strong>Adam Mickiewicz University in Poznań </strong>was <strong>founded in 1919</strong>, <strong>but its traditions go back to the 16</strong><strong><sup>th</sup></strong><strong> c</strong>. It is a <strong>major distinguished research center</strong>, which offers <strong>a variety of study programs</strong> at all levels of tertiary education; it <strong>values the idea of mobility</strong> (Excellent International Student Satisfaction Award granted in 2016), <strong>contributes to the social realities</strong> <strong>of Poznań</strong> and the Greater Poland region. <strong><em>Passion well educated</em></strong> &ndash; <strong>the motto</strong>, which <strong>promotes the University</strong>, highlights its <strong>orientation to competitiveness, innovation and passion for learning</strong>. </p>
