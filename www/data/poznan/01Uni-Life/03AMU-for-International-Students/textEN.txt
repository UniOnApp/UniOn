<p><strong>AMU International Office</strong> is a department of university administration <strong>coordinating international mobility of students and staff</strong>, formally responsible for bi-lateral international agreements and procedures regarding mobility programs. The Erasmus+ Program Office is part of the International Office. <strong>It is in charge of the online Erasmus recruitment, arranging accommodation in residence halls</strong> or assisting with renting, preparing the grand opening of each academic year, providing information about study programs and <strong>the interdisciplinary AMU-PIE program</strong> (study offer of short courses representing various academic subject areas taught in international languages, mostly in English). The Erasmus+ Office cooperates closely with <strong>Faculty Erasmus+ Program coordinators</strong>. To ensure effective communication, the Erasmus+ Office developed <strong>a mobile application (Erasmus App)</strong>, which helps students to keep track of the updates on the most important information and current events in the city. In the app you can find: organizational hints, descriptions of AMU Faculties, links to websites of libraries in Poznań, contact details to Faculty coordinators, AMU-PIE offer of courses (Programs for International Exchange), description of projects run by <strong>Erasmus Student Network</strong>, useful updates on the upcoming cultural events, photos and films promoting AMU, maps identifying your current location and the option of calling a taxi, which will arrive at your location.</p>
<p> To take part in the mobility program and study at AMU for one or two semesters please contact your Faculty coordinator in order <strong>to receive a formal nomination</strong>. You should prepare the required documents and <strong>fill the obligatory online application</strong>, as well as submit the first version of the Learning Agreement. The coordinator of the correspondent AMU Faculty will soon contact you with comments on your proposition, either accepting it or suggesting some alterations. You can check <strong>the status</strong> <strong>of your application online</strong> <strong>(examined, accepted or rejected)</strong>. Remember about the deadlines for submitting your application: for winter semester/ one academic year &ndash; July 15th and for summer semester &ndash; January 15th.</p>
