<p><strong>AMU University Library</strong></p>
<p>In the Neo-Renaissance building built at the beginning of the 20th c. you can find historical collections and collections of books on various other subject areas, as well as a modern online platform enabling a contact with the reader. The electronic databases offer abundant collections of publications in English and in other foreign languages. The librarians who manage collections on particular subject areas and the Research and Reference Information Centre + Catalogue Information will assist you in your search. You may also avail yourself of online support and Ask a librarian service. You may influence the extension of the library collection by suggesting the purchase of a particular book, too. You shouldn&rsquo;t miss the Late-Night Rendez-BU at the University Library. During the examination period it offers the best conditions for learning, atmosphere of concentration, and knowledge within reach, all of this till midnight. The Informatorium, Lending Library and Reading Rooms are open Monday to Friday 9.00 a.m. to 8.00 p.m. and on Saturdays 10.00 a.m. to 5.00 p.m. To use the Library collections, it is necessary to sign in and pay the yearly fee of 12 zł.</p>
