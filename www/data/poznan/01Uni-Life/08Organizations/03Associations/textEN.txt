<p>Active students who wish to develop their interests outside the university courses may also organize themselves in student scholarly associations. Detailed information about those associations is available on the websites of the faculties within the framework of which they operate.</p>
<p>Students may also choose to study abroad within the framework of international exchange programs such as Erasmus+ and the inner mobility program MOST.</p>
