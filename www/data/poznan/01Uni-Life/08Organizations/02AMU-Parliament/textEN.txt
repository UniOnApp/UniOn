<p>If you want to have influence on the shape of the academic community, contact AMU Parliament of Student Self-Government. It is not only entitled to represent students or decide on current administrative and organizational issues, but also may be involved in forming the parameters of education, components of study programs and strategy of development. The Parliament of Student Self-Government gives its opinion on the candidates for AMU Vice-Rector for Student Affairs. The Parliament&rsquo;s standing Commissions (for teaching, legal matters and socio-economic issues) tightly collaborate with the Department of Education, Council for the Quality of Education, Career Office and Faculty administration. The Parliament of Student Self-Government ensures the positive social image of the University and engages in initiatives promoting education, for example within the projects such as AMU Ambassador (educational fairs, presentations about AMU at secondary schools) and Friendly Dean&rsquo;s Office. The Parliament arranges holidays and events for all AMU students: AMU&rsquo;s Grand Barbecue, Student Rag, Sports Days, Festival of Student Culture &ldquo;Culminations&rdquo;.</p>
