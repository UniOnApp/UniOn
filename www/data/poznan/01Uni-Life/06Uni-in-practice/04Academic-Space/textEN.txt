It is always worth to ask for directions in search of the lecture room. In Collegium Maius there is a method of indicating the floor &ndash; the first numeral of the classroom number refers to the floor the room is at, for instance room 222 is at the second floor, while 325 at the third floor. Classrooms named after famous researchers and artists are usually lecture halls large enough to seat all students in a given year.
