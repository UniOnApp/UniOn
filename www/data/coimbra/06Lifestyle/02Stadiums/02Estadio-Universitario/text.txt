<p>The UC stadium is situated in Santa Clara on the left bank of the Mondego river. It's been used by the Coimbra students and population since 1963, and after renovation works the stadium will welcome the European Universities Games in July 2018 (EUSA 2018). 22 different sports can be practiced on its pitches and pavilions. Check them out <a href="https://www.uc.pt/estadiouniversitario/ModalidadesDesportivas">here</a>! From team games and figure skating to athletics, weight lifting and martial arts, professional athletes and sports enthusiasts can meet under its roof. The stadium and gym are open at weekends.</p>
