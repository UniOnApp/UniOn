<p>PSP, or <em>Pol&iacute;cia de Seguran&ccedil;a P&uacute;blica</em> has a mission of safeguarding the internal security and defending citizens&rsquo; rights. In Coimbra, there are uniformed officers patrolling the streets who work to prevent crimes. </p>
<p>If you find yourself in a situation when you don&rsquo;t feel safe or you have witnessed a crime, you can report it by dialing the free emergency number 112. Nearby the main UC campus, there is a police station (<em>Pol&iacute;cia Municipal</em>) at <a href="https://www.cm-coimbra.pt/index.php/servios-municipais-menu-servicos-137/polcia-municipal-menu-servicos-426">Avenida S&aacute; da Bandeira, 106</a>. </p>
