<p><strong>CTT</strong>&nbsp;is the national state-owned Portuguese postal service. It provides express courier, parcel and express mail services tailored to the different needs of customers, and many other different services. Check out the offer available <a href="http://www.ctt.pt/correio-e-encomendas/enviar-correio/opcoes-de-envio/index.html?com.dotmarketing.htmlpage.language=3">here</a>.</p>
<p>There are over 1,000 <em>correios (</em>post offices) in Portugal. In main towns and tourist areas post offices are open on weekdays from 8.30am to 6pm (some are closed for lunch) and on Saturday mornings until 12.30. </p>
<p>The two closest to Polo I post offices are at Papelaria Papiro (Rua Castro Matoso, 12) and Rua Ferreira Borges, 81, but you <a href="http://www.ctt.pt/feapl_2/app/open/stationSearch/stationSearch.jspx?lang=def">can find them all here</a>, with details on opening hours and available services.</p>
