<p>In Portugal, <em>Multibanco</em> means ATM, although it is much more than that:&nbsp;it is a fully integrated interbank network. Once you have opened an account in Portugal, you will be able to use <em>Multibanco</em> machines&rsquo; wide range of services, from paying your household bills, to booking train or concert tickets!
