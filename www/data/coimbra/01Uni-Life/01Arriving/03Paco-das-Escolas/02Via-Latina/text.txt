<p>Via Latina is an imposing collonaded balcony with a central staircase, which connects the Faculty of Law to the rector&rsquo;s office. The university community and alumni, as well as visitors have used it as a favourite spot for group photos. Enter the building behind the staircase, and walk into the Old University most iconic rooms - <em>Sala dos Capelos</em> and <em>Sala do Exame Privado -</em> where official acts and ceremonies take place, such as Ab<em>ertura Solene das Aulas</em>. </p>
