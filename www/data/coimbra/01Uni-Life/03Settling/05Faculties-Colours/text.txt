<p>In UC different colours mean different faculties and you can see this not only during student celebrations, but also in UC merchandising.</p>
<p>Traditionally, students of the penultimate year wear eight large satin ribbons in their <em>pasta acad&eacute;mica</em> in the colours of their school:</p>
<p><span style="background-color: #0000a0; color: #ffffff;"><strong> Letras</strong></span> - Arts and Humanities (dark blue),</p>
<p><span style="background-color: #ff0000; color: #ffffff;"><strong> Direito</strong></span> - Law (red),</p>
<p><span style="background-color: #ffff00;"><strong> Medicina</strong></span> - Medicine (yellow),</p>
<p><span style="background-color: #00ccff;"><strong> Ci&ecirc;ncias e Tecnologia</strong></span> - Sciences and Technology (light blue and white),</p>
<p><span style="background-color: #800080; color: #ffffff;"><strong> Farm&aacute;cia</strong></span> - Pharmacy (purple),</p>
<p><span style="background-color: #ff0000; color: #ffffff;"><strong> Economia</strong></span> - Economics (red and white),</p>
<p><span style="background-color: #ff9900;"><strong>Psicologia e Ci&ecirc;ncias da Educa&ccedil;&atilde;o</strong></span> - Psychology and Educational Sciences (orange),</p>
<p><span style="background-color: #800000; color: #ffffff;"><strong>Ci&ecirc;ncias do Desporto e Educa&ccedil;&atilde;o F&iacute;sica</strong></span> - Sport Sciences and Physical Education (brown)</p>
<p>In their final year, each ribbon is assigned to a specific group of people: professors, parents, fianc&eacute;e/spouse, university acquaintances, friends, relatives, siblings, coursemates. They are to leave a signature and a good wish for the <em>finalista</em>.</p>
