<p>Have you ever had lunch at an imposing 18<sup>th</sup> century cloister? Try the university restaurant <em>Col&eacute;gio de Jesus</em>. It&rsquo;s a treat on sunny days when the patio is open. The lunch buffet of meat, fish and vegetarian dishes, salads, desserts and drinks costs &euro;7. Invite your friends and family - the restaurant is open to the general public and it is the only place working in August. After lunch, visit the two research museums in the same building and check out some zoology and geology historic exhibits. </p>
