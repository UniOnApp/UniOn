<p><em>Monumento aos Mortos da Grande Guerra </em>is a monumental sculptural group in honour of the soldiers of the region who fought and died in the first world war. The first stone was laid on October 5<sup>th</sup>, 1930, and it was inaugurated in 1932.</p>
<p>Standing on a large pedestal with a bronze inscription, the monument consists of solid juxtaposed stone blocks and it shows four soldiers in sentry, holding their guns. It is decorated with national symbols, and topped with a "projectile".</p>
<p>Every year, there is a tribute on November 11<sup>th</sup>, with flowers being laid and a guard of honour.</p>
