<p>Even if the experience of shopping in Baixa has been completely transformed by the shopping centre phenomenon causing the &lsquo;big brands&rsquo; to move their shops away, there&rsquo;s still something very special about wandering the little streets searching for a good deal in shoes, clothes or household items, or just to find a gift.</p>
<p>Rua Ferreira Borges, now filled with souvenir shops and nice caf&eacute;s, is the central artery of Baixa, and well worth a visit. But take some time to explore &lsquo;Baixinha&rsquo;, with its streets named after old craftsmanship like &lsquo;Rua dos Sapateiros&rsquo; (Shoemakers St.)</p>
