<p>To buy a nice outfit could not be easier: Portugal has an abundance of textile and leather factories and producers, which are among the country&rsquo;s lead exports, with high quality products at affordable prices. As one of the largest textile exporters in Europe, several fashion labels you know look to Portugal to sources fabrics.&nbsp;</p>
<p>For centuries Portugal&rsquo;s shoemakers have been crafting some of the finest footwear in Europe, so look for the <em>Made in Portugal</em> label if you&rsquo;re searching for good leather shoe-wear, some eco-friendly cork boots, or a pair of designer shoes.</p>
