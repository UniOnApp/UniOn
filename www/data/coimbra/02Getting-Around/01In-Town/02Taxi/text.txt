<p>If you need a taxi, you can call <a href="http://www.politaxis.pt/contacts.html">Polit&aacute;xis</a> (tel. 239 499 090) or walk to the nearby taxi ranks, in Rua de Tomar and Pra&ccedil;a da Rep&uacute;blica. To get the cost of your trip, you have a Taxi-Link <a href="http://taximeter.geolink.pt/">simulator</a>: enter origin, destination, additional info (such as luggage or pets), choose between day or night rates and add the cost of the call.</p>
