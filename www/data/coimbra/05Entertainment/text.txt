<p>Coimbra as a World Heritage site attracts many tourists and visitors. As a university city, Coimbra offers two types of entertainment: linked to the university history, higher education and scientific knowledge in Portugal, as well as various tourist and cultural attractions. You can get a hands-on experience of some innovative techniques in theatrical, cinema, music and dance performances, get involved in art experimentation, and witness their creative results. &nbsp;</p>
<p>On weekdays, you can do research at one of the university museums and study theatre, dance or cinema. Your weekends, holidays and evenings can be full of activities: go to a theatre performance or to a concert, meet up your friends at a museum or go dancing. Sometimes you don't even have to leave the campus! </p>
