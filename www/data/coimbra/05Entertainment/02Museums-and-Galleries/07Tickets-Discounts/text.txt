<p>Without leaving the UC campus, you can visit several museums. Admittance is free if you study here but enrollment proof is required. Students of other universities and holders of the European Youth card get discounts. Several options of guided tours and group visits are available by <a href="http://www.uc.pt/informacaopara/visit/bilhetes/comuni_uc/">appointment</a>.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Museums in Portugal are usually open until 6-7 pm throughout the year, except for Mondays, Easter, May Day, Christmas, New Year Day and national holidays. Some close for lunch.</p>
<p>Special rates apply for students, holders of the European Youth Card, guided tours and previously booked group and research visits.</p>
<p>Since July 2017, admittance has become free for everyone on Sundays and national holidays until 2pm.</p>
<p>Tickets to national museums in Coimbra, Porto, Lisbon, Mafra, Tomar, Batalha, Alcoba&ccedil;a and Viseu can be bought <a href="http://www.patrimoniocultural.gov.pt/en/museus-e-monumentos/dgpc/bilheteira/">online</a>. Enjoy your visit!</p>
