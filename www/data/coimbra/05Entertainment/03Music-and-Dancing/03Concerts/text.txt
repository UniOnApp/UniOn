<p><a href="http://www.coimbraconvento.pt/pt/">Convento de S&atilde;o Francisco</a> was founded in the early 16<sup>th</sup> century. Throughout its history, it served as hospital and barracks, and housed an important textile-manufacturing unit. Acquired by the Coimbra Municipal Council, the building was recovered and assumes its new vocation as a Cultural and Conference Center.</p>
<p>Teatro Acad&eacute;mico de Gil Vicente, inaugurated in 1961 is a structure of the University of Coimbra. Inaugurated in 1961, its cultural, artistic and educational mission has developed over the years between society and the University, assuming its character as a cultural exception in Portuguese territory, since it is the only university theater building in the country.</p>
